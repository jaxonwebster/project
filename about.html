About Me

I earned my bachelor’s degree in Management Information Systems from Brigham Young University, where I discovered a passion for technology, problem solving, and building systems that make a real impact. I enjoy taking complex challenges and turning them into thoughtful, practical solutions that people can actually use.


I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree at Utah State University with a focus on Web Development and Cybersecurity, with an expected graduation in April 2027. I’ve thrived in the rigorous coursework at both BYU and USU, and I’m especially motivated by environments that demand discipline, curiosity, and continuous learning.


Outside of work and school, I value balance and creativity. I enjoy running, baking bread, watching films, listening to music, and spending time with my wife and our baby boy. These interests keep me grounded and influence the way I approach both problem solving and collaboration.


Looking ahead, my goal is to grow into a leadership role within a technology organization that prioritizes integrity, collaboration, and meaningful impact. I’m motivated by the opportunity to mentor others, contribute to strong teams, and help build technology that aligns with clear values and long-term purpose.
